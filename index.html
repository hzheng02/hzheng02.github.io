BIOGRAPHY

Harry Zheng is a highly dedicated and passionate musician, striving to become a professional clarinetist and educator. Currently a fourth-year performance major at the Don Wright Faculty Music from Western University in London, Ontario, he has gained numerous performance, teaching, and research opportunities as a student of Dr. Jana Starling. In recent years, Harry has performed with the Symphonic Band, Wind Ensemble, and Opera during his time at Western University, and was also appointed Principal Clarinet with the Mississauga Symphony Youth Orchestra. In addition, he has worked with Dr. Starling on a variety of tasks such as data entry for the ICA, administrative support with creating programs, and aiding with co-hosting residency guests at the music department.

A fervent believer in community service, Harry is not only diligent in departmental work, but he also teaches clarinet as a clarinet mentor. As students came out of the pandemic during what was an especially unfavourable time for wind music education, clarinet majors at Western offered free clarinet instruction to support band programs in Ontario. The program has continued since the pandemic, allowing him to develop and demonstrate his sense of strong leadership and contribution as a nurturing mentor to young aspiring musicians.

With an extensive range of solo, orchestral, and chamber performance experiences, combined with his passion to advocate for music education, the synergy between performance and pedagogy continues to shape his approach to music.

AWARDS

    2023-NOV

    Young-Ja Park Chamber Music Award
    Awarded annually to full-time Don Wright Faculty of Music graduate and/or undergraduate students performing in a Chamber Music group, with a minimum 70% average, who are being recognized as members of the most promising Chamber Music Group, and exemplify excellence in performance and collaborative work throughout the fall term.

    2023-NOV

    London Music Scholarship Foundation Endowment Awards
    Awarded annually to undergraduate or graduate students entering or currently registered in applied studies in the Don Wright Faculty of Music who have been identified as excellent performers in annual competition(s) which may include auditions or juries, conducted by the Faculty of Music.

    2023-NOV

    Jack Newbegin Award in Music
    Awarded annually to a full-time undergraduate Music student, in any year, who is majoring in an orchestral instrument, organ or piano, and has demonstrated financial need and academic achievement.

    2023-NOV

    Music Industries Association of Canada Award
    Awarded annually to a full-time undergraduate student in third or fourth year enrolled in the Don Wright Faculty of Music, with preference given to a student registered in the Specialization in Music Administrative Studies (MAS), with a minimum 70% average, and demonstrated financial need.

    2022-SEP

    Herman Smith Award in Music
    Awarded to a student in Year 2 or beyond in the Don Wright Faculty of Music, based on academic achievement and demonstrated financial need.

    2020-SEP

    Music Entrance Scholarship of Merit
    Awarded to full-time students entering the first year of undergraduate studies in the Don Wright Faculty of Music, based on a combination of performance audition and academic excellence.
